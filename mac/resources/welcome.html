Gnosis VPN Client Installer

Welcome! This installer will set up the Gnosis VPN on your Mac, providing you with decentralized VPN connectivity through the HOPR network.

What will be installed:

✓ Gnosis VPN app
✓ Gnosis VPN system service

System Requirements:

  • macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later
  • Approximately 100MB of available disk space
